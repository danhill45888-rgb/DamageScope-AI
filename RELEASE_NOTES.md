 Overview

This release represents the official OpenAI Build Week contest build of DamageScope AI, an AI-assisted forensic building inspection and documentation platform designed to help property professionals capture, organize, analyze, and document property damage using a structured, evidence-first workflow.

DamageScope AI combines decades of real-world insurance adjusting and building-damage expertise with OpenAI GPT-5.6 and Codex to improve inspection consistency, evidence organization, professional reporting, and forensic reasoning.

 Known Limitations

This repository represents the OpenAI Build Week contest build, not a finished commercial release.

Current limitations may include:

* Mobile workflow continues active development.
* Mobile-to-desktop synchronization is continuing to mature.
* Additional building-component libraries are planned.
* Expanded damage-reference datasets remain under development.
* Commercial authentication and deployment are not included in the contest build.
* LiDAR measurement capabilities are planned for future releases.
* Advanced integrations with third-party estimating and claims platforms are planned.
* AI-assisted findings require review by a qualified professional and are not intended to replace expert judgment.
